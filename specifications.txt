The game requires 2 players: Computer and Human. Each one selects 1 move, each move has an integer value.

Possible moves:
  Rock = 1
  Paper = 2
  Scissors = 3

Combinations:
  Rock wins Scissors and looses to Paper
  Paper wins Rock and looses to Scissors
  Scissors wins Paper and looses to Rock

Human player enters his/her move by sending SMS to +37258821021 with Rock, Paper or Scissors typed in to the SMS.
Computer player randomly generates a value from 1 to 3.

The moves are compared and the result is sent to the human player's phone number.
